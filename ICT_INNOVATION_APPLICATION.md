**Title of Innovation*** (0/50 words)
**SMS - School Management System: Integrated Digital Platform for Educational Administration**

**Brief Description*** (0/200 words)

SMS is a comprehensive web-based school management system designed to modernize educational administration across schools in developing regions. The platform provides integrated modules for:

- **Student & Staff Management**: Complete personnel records, enrollment tracking, and role-based access control
- **Academic Operations**: Exam creation, grading, result management, and transcript generation
- **Attendance Tracking**: Automated attendance records with reporting capabilities
- **Financial Management**: Fees collection, payment tracking, expense management, and financial reporting
- **Communication Hub**: Notifications for parents, students, and staff with multi-channel delivery
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Comprehensive dashboards with exportable reports in multiple formats (PDF, Excel, CSV)

The system features a modern React-based frontend with a robust Express.js backend API, MongoDB database, and role-based access control (RBAC) with 7+ user roles including admin, teachers, students, parents, and accountants. Currently serving multiple schools with 1000+ users.
